  5. Click Manual
  6. Enter Serial Code:
  7. Click Next
  8. Click Copy button
  9. Open sites: https://www.almaftuchin.com/activate-nitro-pro-13

    • Enter form by following guide:
    • Order ID: Enter your 15 Digit Order ID

  12. Back to Nitro Pro 13
  13. Click Next
  14. Enter the activation code you received in the email
